Rules for Tutors

Before registering as a tutor with Scholars Tuition Media, please read and agree to the following rules carefully.

📜

These rules are in place to ensure fairness, trust, and transparency between tutors, students, parents, and Scholars Tuition Media. By registering with us, you agree to follow all the terms listed below. Violation of these rules may result in removal from our platform.

01
Media Service Charge — 50% of First Month
Once you are successfully placed with a student through Scholars Tuition Media, you are required to pay 50% of your first month's salary as a service charge to the media. This fee covers the cost of finding, verifying, and connecting you with the student.
💰 Example: If monthly salary = ৳3,000 → Pay ৳1,500 to media
02
Refund if Student Cancels in First Month
If the parent or guardian cancels the tuition within the first month for any reason not caused by the tutor (e.g. family situation, relocation, financial reasons), the media will refund the service charge paid by the tutor — either fully or partially based on how many days classes were held.
✅ Refund processed within 7 working days
03
No Refund if Tutor Quits First Month
If the tutor voluntarily quits or abandons the tuition within the first month without a valid reason, no refund will be issued. The media reserves the right to blacklist the tutor from future placements.
❌ Service charge is non-refundable in this case
04
Direct Dealing Without Media is Prohibited
Tutors must not bypass the media to deal directly with students or parents found through our platform. If discovered, the tutor will be permanently removed from the media and may face legal notice. All initial arrangements must go through Scholars Tuition Media.
⚠️ This is a strict zero-tolerance rule
05
Provide Accurate Personal Information
Tutors must submit accurate and honest information during registration — including their real name, educational qualifications, institution, subject expertise, and contact details. Providing false information will result in immediate removal from the platform.
📋 NID / Student ID may be required for verification
06
Maintain Punctuality & Professionalism
Tutors are expected to arrive on time, be respectful to students and families, and maintain a professional attitude at all times. Repeated complaints about behavior or irregular attendance may result in suspension from the media.
🕐 Inform in advance if you must miss a class
07
Salary Negotiation via Media Only
The monthly salary rate must be agreed upon and finalized through the media before classes begin. Tutors should not independently demand extra fees from students or parents beyond the agreed amount without informing the media first.
📞 Contact media for any salary change requests
08
Report Problems to Media Immediately
If any issue arises — such as the student not paying salary, inappropriate behavior from the family, or safety concerns — the tutor must report it to the media immediately. Do not handle disputes alone. The media will mediate and take necessary action.
🚨 We are always here to support our tutors

📌 Important Notice: By registering with Scholars Tuition Media and accepting a tuition placement, you automatically agree to all the rules listed above.

⚖️ Dispute Resolution: Any dispute between tutor and student/family will be reviewed and decided by Scholars Tuition Media. Our decision will be considered final.

📞 Questions? If you have any questions about these rules before registering, please contact us via WhatsApp or call us directly. We're happy to clarify.
